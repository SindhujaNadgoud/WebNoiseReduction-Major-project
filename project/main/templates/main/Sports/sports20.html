Basketball is a team game played on a court. Each five-person team attempts to throw or dunk an inflated ball into the opponent's basket, which is mounted on a backboard that is ten feet above the floor. The International Basketball Federation (FIBA) is the recognized governing body for basketball world wide. Founded in 2002, FIBA aims to create a worldwide basketball network to achieve a sustainable continuous growth of the sport of basketball.
